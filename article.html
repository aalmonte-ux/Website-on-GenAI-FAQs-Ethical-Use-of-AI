Wedding Documentation Reflection

You are organizing your wedding, and the documentation team is asking 40k for the package. They plan to use an iPhone 16 Pro Max and CapCut for same-day video edits. The team has 2 years of experience and an above-average portfolio.

Integrity

The team demonstrates integrity by being responsible for the quality and accuracy of their work. Just like skilled developers overseeing AI-assisted tasks, they are accountable for delivering a polished final product that meets expectations.

Creativity

Using mobile technology and CapCut creatively allows the team to produce professional results efficiently. This mirrors the balance of human insight and technology seen in AI-assisted work, where innovation enhances output without replacing expertise.

Skill Level

Two years of experience with an above-average portfolio indicate competent skill. Like “vibe” coders using AI responsibly, the team can make thoughtful decisions, customize the final product, and ensure high-quality results that justify their fee.

Conclusion

Agreeing to the 40k package is reasonable. The cost reflects not just the tools but the combination of integrity, creativity, and skill. The team’s expertise ensures reliable, ethical, and polished documentation, similar to how human oversight ensures quality in AI-assisted projects.
